Goal

The universe is the result of a highly creative and efficient process, capable of performing the greatest complexity with minimum effort. Behind this process is an inherently self-organizing, self-regulating, self-healing, self-beautifying, and self-creating pattern.

Central question: Why is it that the human brain does not consistently perform at a similar level of creativity and efficiency as the process that created it?

This work explores how humanity became misaligned with universal processes and identifies where a paradigm shift must begin to restore that alignment.

To get started, we suggest you watch the animated short film, and then explore the Q&A Introduction and additional chapters. You are invited to flow with your curiosity via the hyperlinks to explore in detail the concepts presented in this work.
