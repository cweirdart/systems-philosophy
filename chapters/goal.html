Goal

The universe is the result of a highly creative and efficient process, capable of performing the greatest complexity with minimum effort. Behind this process is an inherently self-organizing, self-regulating, self-healing, self-beautifying, and self-creating pattern.

So as a byproduct of this process…

Why is it that the human brain does not consistently perform at a similar level of creativity and efficiency as the process that created it?

This work explores how we became out of sync with the universal process, and where the paradigm shift starts in order for us to realign with it again.

For better understanding, we suggest you watch the animated short film, and then explore the Q&A Introduction and additional chapters that interest you most.

Throughout this site, you are invited to flow with your curiosity via the hyperlinks to explore in detail the concepts and theories that together are offering us a route through a major paradigm shift.
