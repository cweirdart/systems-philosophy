Chapter 7

What is the Principle Quality or Underlying Feeling of the Universe?

Natural systems demonstrate unconditional love as a fundamental universal principle. Earth and the Sun model resource-sharing without conditions — the Sun provides energy regardless of how recipients use it. Dogs exemplify this quality, generating abundant affection while consuming minimal resources.

Spiritual Experience and Unity

Spiritual experiences across various practices reveal a common element: transcendence of separateness and unity with larger systems produces feelings of love, peace, and calm. This state represents the pinnacle of human emotional experience.

Love as Creation's Core

Most spiritual and mythological traditions emphasize love as creation's core purpose. Christianity embodies this through Christ's teachings. Sufism teaches that the creator, being love itself, manifested physically to experience and suffer through human consciousness, enabling a richer expression of divine love.

"Through Love all that is bitter will sweeten. Through Love all that is copper will be gold. Through Love all dregs will turn to purest wine. Through Love all pain will turn to medicine. Through Love the dead will all become alive. Through Love the king will turn into a slave!"

— Rumi
