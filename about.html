About

This Work

This project explores a nonlinear worldview suggesting we inhabit a self-organizing universe, bridging philosophy, science, and spirituality through over 30 years of research.

The initiative aims to shift humanity's perception from linear, separation-based thinking to nonlinear understanding of our interconnectedness.

Mission

  • Transitioning from linear to nonlinear worldviews using network, systems, and cognitive theories
  • Recognizing universal behavioral patterns in nonlinear networks applicable across domains
  • Identifying solutions to personal, social, environmental, and global challenges
  • Bridging science and spirituality through systems understanding

"I see this as the knowledge of the Earth. It doesn't belong to me; but it is precious to me because it has changed my life."

— Mansoor Vakili
